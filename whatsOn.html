CURRENT EXHIBITIONS

EXHIBITION

SEE ME, SEE YOU: EARLY VIDEO INSTALLATION OF SOUTHEAST ASIA

05 MAY 2023 - 04 FEB 2024 | ALL DAY

Video installation is a common form of contemporary art today. Yet, before the sleek projection technologies which dominate our present-day encounter of this form, these early video installation works emerged across Southeast Asia in the 1980s and 1990s.

EXHIBITION

LIU KUO-SUNG:EXPERIMENTATION AS METHOD

13 JAN 2023 - 25 FEB 2024 | ALL DAY

Liu Kuo-sung: Experimentation as Method is the largest exhibition by a Singapore public museum dedicated to the Chinese ink master, Liu Kuo-sung, which celebrates his artistic career spanning 70 years.

EXHIBITION

THE NEGLETED DIMENSION

07 JUL 2023 - 25 FEB 2024 | ALL DAY

The Neglected Dimension explores the period from the 1960s onward when calligraphy and abstraction became prominent modes of artistic production in Islamic art in Indonesia. Explore a range of modern Islamic artistic expressions from the early pioneers of Indonesian abstract art to explorations with new mediums and contemporary performance art.

EXHIBITION

TROPICAL: STORIES FROM SOUTHEAST ASIA AND LATIN AMERICA

18 NOV 2023 - 24 MAR 2024 | ALL DAY

Tropical: Stories from Southeast Asia and Latin America will be the first large-scale museum exhibition to take a comparative approach across two regions united by their shared struggles against colonialism. Comprising over 200 paintings, sculptures, prints and immersive installations, Tropical spans the 20th century, tracing how artists from Southeast Asia and Latin America forged connections and nurtured solidarities, defiantly reclaiming their place within the story of art.

EXHIBITION

GALLERY'S CHILDREN BIENNALE 2023: LET'S MAKE A BETTER PLACE

27 MAR 2023 - 31 MAR 2024 | ALL DAY

The Biennale’s theme of Let's Make A Better Place aims to allow children to discover their better place—one they can create for themselves as they grow in their journey of understanding not only their own thoughts, feelings, motives, and wants, but those in others as well.
